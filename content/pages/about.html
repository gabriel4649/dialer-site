Koipun is more than just about learning Japanese. It is also about helping people realize their full potential, give them access to new worlds, learn self-discipline, make new friends and last but not least have fun! Finally, we believe that by getting to know other cultures we learn more about ourselves and become more tolerant, loving and understanding.

Our Story

こんにちは、みなさん。 私は ガブリエルです。 I started Koipun right after graduating in 2014 from Georgia Tech where I earned a Master's degree in Computer Science.

I have had a life long passion for Japanese culture. Because of that I have tried to learn Japanese many times, but had always failed, until I started taking Japanese at Georgia Tech, where I became enamored with the class and started making progress very fast.

After taking Professor Masato Kikuchi's online version of the class I got the idea of making this accessible to all Japanese learners around the globe, and here we are now!
